Study choice

I completed a 5-year high school programme, specialising in Computer Techniques and Technologies. From that program I got a somewhat advanced level of knowledge about computers - what they are and how they work.

Why I chose ICT programme

I chose to continue specialising in computers as it has been my passion even before attending high school. The HZ ICT programme offers everything that I look for in a study programme:

  • Programming assignments
  • Internship
  • Like-minded people and on top of that - international

After completing the study, I'd like to become a full-time back-end developer for any company until I reach enough capital to open up my own company and offer what the market needs at the specific point in time. :)
