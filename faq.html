1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

  • You must register using your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer.
  • Next, select printer option Print / Afdrukken.In the menu, select the multifunctional printer to where the job has been sent.
  • Next, you can see which printer job is ready to be printed.
  • The print job is the completed provided there is sufficient credit on your printing account.
  • When the printing is completed, press Stop on the TouchID, followed by Logout.

2. How can you scan a document a send it to your laptop at HZ?

  • You must register using your HZ pass on the TouchID next to the multifunctional printer.
  • Next, select scanner option scan / Scannen. In the menu, select the multifunctional scanner to where the job has been sent.
  • Next, you can see which scanner job is ready to be scanned.
  • The scan job is the completed provided there is sufficient credit on your printing account.
  • When the scanning is completed, press Stop on the TouchID, followed by Logout.
